## Getting Started

### 1 — Dossier Tab
Fill in your **Agent Name**, **Level**, **Class**, **Species**, **Archetype**, and **Background**. Your species trait, background feature, and starting equipment populate automatically when you make each selection.

Set your **Faction Standings** (−3 Hated → 0 Neutral → +3 Ally) and choose a **Funding State**. Funding governs downtime access, NPC attitudes, and activates the **Strapped Edge** if you're between jobs.

### 2 — Stats Tab
Enter your four ability scores (**Fortitude · Reflex · Intellect · Presence**). All derived values — Defense, saves, attack modifiers, Scaling Threat DC, and resource pool maximums — update automatically.

Use the **Combat Band** at the top to track HP and class resources (VP / GP / TP) during play. The **+/−** buttons apply damage or healing by a typed amount. **Short Rest** restores half HP and half resources (once per Long Rest). **Long Rest** restores everything and clears conditions.

Toggle active **Conditions** and track **Death Saving Throws** (1d20, rolled privately) at the bottom of the tab.

### 3 — Skills Tab
Allocate **Skill Points** using the budget tracker. You receive **4 + INT modifier** points at Level 1, then **INT modifier per level** (minimum 1; Automs gain +1 per level). No skill can exceed **Level + 1** ranks. Total check = **2d10 + Skill Rank + Ability Modifier**.

| Difficulty | DC |
|---|---|
| Standard | 13–16 |
| Hard | 17–20 |
| Very Hard | 21–25 |
| Extreme | 26–29 |
| Legendary | 30 |

### 4 — Powers Tab
Select your known powers, techniques, equipment, and modifications using the **checkboxes**. Each checked item costs **1 Customization Slot**. The slot tracker at the top of the tab shows your budget (**Level + 1** slots total).

**Defensive Loadouts** (Gunslingers) and **Chassis Configurations** (Automs) each cost 1 slot if a non-default option is chosen. Your **archetype's base abilities** auto-populate in the Actions Reference table when you select an archetype on the Dossier tab.

**Void Augmentations** are visible only when the **Essent Conduit** archetype is selected.

> **Upcasting:** Spend double VP to double the primary effect — doubled damage dice, doubled duration, or doubled targets.

### 5 — Gear Tab
Log your weapons (damage dice, type, attribute, rarity, and special properties), track consumable quantities with the **+/−** buttons, and record your ship, vehicle, and equipment notes.

**Damage type quick reference:**
- ⬛ **Kinetic** — no bonus interactions
- ⬟ **Energy** — environmental resistance varies
- ⬡ **Ion** — +1d8 vs. Automs and mechanical systems
- ⬢ **Void** — +1d6 vs. biological creatures

### 6 — Notes Tab
Free-text fields for Backstory, Motivation, Campaign Notes, NPC Contacts, Faction Notes, and Miscellaneous.

## Saving Your Sheet

Your sheet **auto-saves to your browser's local storage** on every input change. Use the **Save** button in the nav bar for a manual save with confirmation. To back up or share your character, use your browser's **Print** button (or Ctrl/Cmd + P) to export a clean printable version.

> ⚠ Clearing your browser data or using a different browser/device will not carry your character over. For persistence across devices, use the Print function to save a PDF.

## System Reference

| Mechanic | Rule |
|---|---|
| Attack rolls | 2d10 + modifier — all attacks connect |
| Glancing Blow | Roll ≤ 12 — half damage before Defense |
| Solid Hit | Roll 13–17 — full damage minus Defense |
| Critical Hit | Natural 18–20 — half dice maximized + half rolled, or choose a Stunt |
| Short Rest | Half max HP + half max resources · once per Long Rest |
| Long Rest | Full HP + full resources + all Once/Long Rest abilities |
| Funding: Strapped Edge | Once per combat, treat a Glancing Blow as a Solid Hit · +1 resource at combat start |
